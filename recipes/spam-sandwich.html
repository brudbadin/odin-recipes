Spam Sandwich

Description

A simple yet filling sandwich great for a budget meal. The salty spam paired with delicious toast and cheese pairs perfectly with mac and sauce!

Ingredients

  • 2-4 1/4" Thick Slices Spam
  • 2 Slices Bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to a boil. Once boiling, add about 1 tablespoon salt to the water.
  2. Add noodles and cook according to package directions, or until al-dante.
  3. Once cooked, drain water from noodles and return them to the pot.
  4. Add equal parts tomato sauce and water to the pot with the noodles. We usually just fill the empty can of tomato sauce with water.
  5. Add the bacon grease or oil to the pot and return to heat. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Serve mac and sauce in a bowl, and top with salt, pepper, and natures seasoning to taste. Enjoy!
