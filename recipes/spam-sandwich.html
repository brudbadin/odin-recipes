Spam Sandwich

Description

A simple yet filling sandwich great for a budget meal. The salty spam paired with delicious toast and cheese pairs perfectly with mac and sauce!

Ingredients

  • 2-4 1/4" Thick Slices Spam
  • 2 Slices Bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. In a frying pan, cook sliced spam to desired crispiness.
  2. Toast sliced bread if desired.
  3. On one slice of bread, add mustard, mayonnaise, sliced cheese, and cooked spam slices. Top with the other slice of bread. Enjoy!
